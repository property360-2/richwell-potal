## 🚀 What to Test After Seeding

1. **Log in as `dean`** → Go to **Section Generation**
2. Select program, term **2026-1**, and a year level (e.g. Year 1)
3. Click **Generate Sections** — it should compute sections based on 70 APPROVED students
4. Click **Auto-Generate Schedule** (optional) to fill in time slots/rooms/professors
5. **Publish the schedule** to open the Section Picking window for students
6. **Log in as a student** (e.g. `270001`) → Go to **Section Picking**
7. Pick preferred session (AM/PM for regular students)

---

## ⚠️ Notes

- The system will create **2 sections per 70 students** (35 per section — within the 40 cap).
- Professors for each year level are pre-assigned to their respective year's subjects.
- `Year 1` professors teach `AM` and `PM` sessions; `Year 3/4` professors are more restricted (part-time).
- All historical grades use `final_grade = 1.50` for simplicity.
